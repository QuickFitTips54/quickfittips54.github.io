Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

At QuickFit Tips, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at https://quickfittips54.github.io.

1. Information We Collect

We use third-party services like Google AdSense and Google Analytics to enhance your experience. These services may collect:

  • Cookies and usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to serve personalized ads and analyze site performance.
  • Anonymous identifiers to track user interactions for ad optimization.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email) unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via contact form).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior to improve content.
  • Ensure the website functions properly.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our site uses cookies from Google AdSense and Google Analytics to personalize ads and track usage. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings or opt out of personalized ads at Google’s Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdSense: Serves ads based on your interests. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymous site usage. See their data practices.

5. Data Security

We rely on GitHub Pages for hosting, which uses industry-standard security measures. However, no online platform is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data protection.

6. Your Rights

You can disable cookies via your browser or opt out of personalized ads. Contact us for any privacy concerns (see below).

7. Contact Us

For questions about this policy, email us at support@quickfittips.com.
